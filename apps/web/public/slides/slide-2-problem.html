The Problem

Neural data sharing is broken.

🔓

No Encryption

EEG recordings sit on centralized servers in plaintext. One breach exposes thousands of brain scans.

🚫

No Access Control

Once shared, data is open to anyone. Researchers have no say in who uses their neural recordings.

💸

No Monetization

Contributors get nothing. The value of their data flows to platforms, not the people who created it.

🔍

No Discovery

Finding the right dataset is manual and slow. Most neural data never gets shared or found at all.
